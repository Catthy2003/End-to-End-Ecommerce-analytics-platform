# Business Requirement

## Dashboard Name
Executive Sales Dashboard

## Target Users
- CEO
- Sales Manager
- Business Analyst

## Objective
Provide a high-level overview of business performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.

## Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value
- Returning Customer Rate
- Average Delivery Time
- Average Review Score

## Filters
- Date
- State
- Product Category
- Payment Type
